Validation Targets

Calculator Checks
  • Only OWCS map-pool maps can be selected.
  • First ban and follow-up ban must use different role groups.
  • A team cannot repeat its own earlier ban in the same series.
  • Banned heroes apply to both teams for the current game.

Team names come from OWCS Korea only. Player rosters stay in OWCStats, but this calculator uses only region, team, hero, and map reference data.
